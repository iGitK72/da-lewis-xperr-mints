Traction Club is an annual competition for a bonus cash prize to the idea or project that receives the most traction throughout the year.

It works by the teams that want to join pay to a pool. This pool builds through the year by all the projects that wish to be considered in the club.

The idea or project can be a campaign, facebook ad, youtube, vimeo, startup mvp or company which proves the most value adding traction on their project wins the cash prize at the end of the year.
